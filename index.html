This is Shrek.

Shrek is a big, green ogre who might look scary at first, but he’s actually a misunderstood hero with a heart of gold! He became a superstar because he showed everyone that you don’t have to be a perfect prince to be the hero of your own story.

Shrek comes from this movie

What makes up Shrek?

  1. Physical Features: Green skin, trumpet-shaped ears, a large nose.
  2. Ogre Physiology: Immense superhuman strength and a powerful roar.
  3. Attire: A beige tunic, a brown scale vest, and plaid pants.
