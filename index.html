Who is Shrek?

This is Shrek.

Shrek is a big, green ogre who might look scary at first, but he’s actually a misunderstood hero with a heart of gold! He became a superstar because he showed everyone that you don’t have to be a perfect prince to be the hero of your own story. Along with his talkative and super funny best friend Donkey, Shrek shows us that true love is about accepting others for who they are, rather than focusing on outward appearance.

What makes up Shrek?

  1. Physical Features: Green skin, trumpet-shaped ears, a large nose, brown eyes, and a large mouth.
  2. Ogre Physiology: Immense superhuman strength, enhanced durability, and a powerful roar.
  3. Attire: A beige tunic, a brown scale vest, and plaid pants.
  4. Personality: Asocial, grumpy, and protective of his privacy initially, later becoming a loyal friend and loving husband/father.
  5. Abilities/Skills: Expert survivalist, hand-to-hand combatant, and expert tactician.
  6. Background: He is an ogre who lives in a swamp and was originally rejected by society before becoming a respected hero.
